Hypnotherapy

Whether you are completely new to hypnotherapy or a life-long advocate, this website will hopefully provide you with the answers to any questions you have.

I have provided an FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions) page which provides answers to some of the most commonly asked questions such as ‘What if I get stuck in hypnosis?’ or ‘Will it work for me?’. There is also information on me, your Therapist , and my qualifications and personal motivations for practising hypnotherapy.

Hypnosis is a non-invasive therapeutic intervention, that sits under the Psychological Therapies umbrella. It is perhaps best described as both a science and an art. If you are interested in this aspect of hypnosis, then you can check out my page on The Mind-Body Link . If you are more interested in the results it can produce, then you may like to check out my Feedback page.
